HOTEL INFORMATION

Hotel Details

  • The name of the hotel is Batty Langley's
  • The hotel’s address is: 12 Folgate Street, London, E1 6BX
  • The hotel’s contact number is: 020 7377 4390

Check-In & Check-Out

  • The check-in time is at 3pm. We do not guarantee early check-in.
  • The check-out time is 12pm. We do not guarantee late check-out
  • Additional fees of up to 100% will be charged for departures after the scheduled time, unless a late check-out has been arranged in advance

Reservations

  • Reservations must be secured at the time of booking by providing debit or credit card details

Cancellations (individual reservtions)

  • Any amendment, cancellation or non-arrival made after 12.00pm (hotel time) 2 days prior to the date of stay will be subject to a one-night charge being made against the card provided

Cancellations (group reservations - 5 rooms or more)

  • We operate a 7 day cancellation policy
  • Any non-arrival, amendment or cancellation made after 12.00pm (hotel time), 7 days prior to the date of arrival will be subject to the full charge of the entire pre booked stay

Cancellations (longer length of stay reservations - 7 nights or more )

  • We operate a 7 day cancellation policy on longer length of stay reservations
  • Any non-arrival, amendment or cancellation made after 12.00pm (hotel time), 7 days prior to the date of stay will be subject to the full charge of the first 7 nights

Room Types

  • The room types offered are: Club Double, Superior Double, Luxury Double, Deluxe Double, Deluxe Double with Terrace, Junior Suite, Kitty Fisher Suite and Earl of Bolingbroke Suite
  • None of the rooms feature twin beds

Room’s Maximum Capacity

  • Club Double: 2 people
  • Superior Double: 2 people
  • Luxury Double: 3 people (with an extra bed)
  • Deluxe Double: 3 people (with an extra bed)
  • Deluxe Double with Terrace: 3 people (with an extra bed)
  • Junior Suite: 4 people (with an extra bed)
  • Kitty Fisher Suite: 4 people (with an extra bed)
  • Earl of Bolingbroke Suite: 3 people (with an extra bed)

Extra Beds

  • Extra beds and baby cots are available on request.
  • Up to 2 children will be accommodated free of charge when sharing a room with an adult (specific rooms only)
  • For persons aged 13 years and above, extra beds will be charged at the rate of £45.00 per night

Room Features Requests

  • We cannot guarantee specific rooms identified by name or color
  • Room features such as baths, walk-in showers, room decor, views, etc., are not guaranteed

SERVICES

Restaurants

  • We can provide restaurant recommendations
  • We do not make restaurant reservations for guests

Parking

  • The hotel does not offer parking facilities
  • We can send a PDF document via email, listing the closest multi-story car parks in the area

Flowers

  • We can provide flower arrangements
  • Guests must ensure they place their flower order at least 48 hours in advance
  • Guests can choose from two bouquet sizes: "Standard" priced at £35 or "Premium" at £65
